It's solving a really simple and common problem. Group of friends go for a trip together, spending days or weeks together. There is a bunch of shared costs, which may include fuel, accommodation, groceries, etc.

It may become tedious to split the shared costs evenly, and the payments necessary to get even. Write the shared costs down here, and we'll calculate the payments for you!

Once you wrote down all the shared costs, you can then click Save button, and send a link to your friends. You can copy the link when you click the Share button. Additionally there's another link, that you can use to edit the bill.
